Our System

The AdvixxX Ad Funnel

A proven 6-step system we deploy for every client to turn cold audiences into raving customers.

01

Audience Research & Targeting

We deep-dive into your ideal customer — demographics, psychographics, interests, and pain points — then build laser-targeted audiences using Meta's advanced targeting tools, custom audiences, and lookalikes.

02

Creative Development (UGC & Static)

We produce multiple creative variations — UGC videos, static images, carousels — engineered with scroll-stopping hooks, strong CTAs and clear value propositions to maximise CTR.

03

TOFU — Awareness Campaigns

We launch broad awareness campaigns targeting cold audiences to build recognition, interest, and initial engagement at the lowest possible CPM and CPC.

04

MOFU — Consideration & Lead Gen

Warm traffic is retargeted with deeper offers — lead magnets, webinars, free audits, or product demos — to move prospects closer to a purchase decision.

05

BOFU — Conversion Campaigns

Hot leads are hit with high-intent conversion ads: strong offers, urgency, social proof and direct purchase CTAs. This is where we drive ROAS.

06

Scale, Optimise & Retain

Once profitable, we systematically scale winning ad sets, kill losers, introduce new creatives, and build retention campaigns (email/WhatsApp) to maximise LTV.
